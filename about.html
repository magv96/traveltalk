About Me

I enjoy travelling the world, getting to know people and cultures. I have been around Europe, the Carribbean, the Americas, and once to the Middle East. I hope that through my exploration of new cultures I can understand other ways of thinking, help others expand their horizons, and be a catalyst for responsible and respectful travel.

I provide an honest account of my experiences to show that travel is not all Insta-perfect posts, nor is it a dangerous endeavour. Travel is the act of opening yourself up to the world and allowing the unknown to teach you about yourself, challenge your beliefs, and remind you of what matters to you. I also provide an honest account because I believe that being transparent about the cost of travel is important. I choose to travel spending more in that section of my life than in other areas. For me saving $20 by not ordering take-out is a conscious decision to pay for a bus ticket to New York or a dinner in Barcelona. I list prices for as many trips as I can.
